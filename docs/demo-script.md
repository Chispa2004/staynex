# Staynex Commercial Demo Script

Goal: show Staynex as an AI Hotel Operations Platform in 5 minutes.

## Before the Call

1. Open `/dashboard/onboarding`.
2. Go to **Test flow**.
3. Click **Generate commercial showcase**.
4. Open `/dashboard` and enable **Demo Mode**.
5. Keep `/dashboard/inbox`, `/dashboard/reservations`, `/dashboard/guest-memory`, and `/dashboard/upsells` ready in browser tabs.

## 5-Minute Story

### 1. PMS Connected

Start on the Executive Dashboard.

Say: "Staynex connects to the hotel's PMS and turns reservations into WhatsApp-ready guest journeys."

Show:
- PMS Status.
- Reservations KPI.
- Automations scheduled.

### 2. Reservation Arrives

Open Reservations.

Show:
- Laura Garcia anniversary stay.
- John Smith late checkout candidate.
- Michael Brown VIP repeat guest.
- WhatsApp token/link generated by Staynex.

### 3. Guest Opens WhatsApp

Open Inbox.

Show:
- Guest conversations ordered by latest activity.
- Clean chat layout.
- AI Copilot button.

Say: "The guest simply writes on WhatsApp. Reception sees the full context."

### 4. AI Concierge Handles the Request

Open Laura Garcia conversation.

Show:
- Anniversary message.
- AI response.
- AI Copilot context.

Wow moment: Staynex understands a romantic stay and suggests a high-value but natural concierge offer.

### 5. Revenue AI

Open Upsells or Executive Dashboard Revenue panel.

Show:
- Romantic package.
- Late checkout.
- Airport transfer.
- Estimated revenue and conversion status.

Say: "This is not just automation. It is measurable revenue attribution."

### 6. Operations Alert

Open Emma Wilson conversation or Tickets.

Show:
- Noise complaint.
- Reception escalation.
- Complaint ticket.

Say: "Staynex separates revenue opportunities from operational risk and routes each one correctly."

### 7. Guest Memory

Open Guest Memory.

Show:
- VIP / romantic / family / late checkout signals.
- Michael Brown VIP profile.

Say: "The AI does not forget. It builds a persistent hospitality memory per guest."

### 8. Executive View

Return to Executive Dashboard.

Show:
- Live AI Activity.
- VIP Guests & AI Signals.
- Revenue & Upsells panel.
- AI Insights.

Close: "Staynex turns WhatsApp into the operating layer for guest service, revenue and hotel intelligence."

## Wow Moments to Prioritize

- AI detects anniversary and creates romantic package opportunity.
- Late checkout creates revenue attribution.
- Noise complaint creates operational escalation.
- VIP repeat guest appears in AI guest signals.
- Executive Dashboard shows revenue and operations in one place.

## Demo Scenarios Included

- Romantic Couple: Laura Garcia.
- Late Checkout: John Smith.
- Noise Complaint: Emma Wilson.
- VIP Guest: Michael Brown.
- Family Traveler: Rodriguez Family.

## Notes for Pilots

- Demo data is connected to real Staynex tables.
- PMS, WhatsApp, AI, revenue, guest memory and automations are represented through the same architecture used by production flows.
- Future PMS confirmations can replace estimated revenue with real folio revenue.
